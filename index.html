Welcome to Mitchel Grand


Mitchell Grand Hotel is offering accommodation in Kaduwela. Featuring a 24-hour front desk, this property also provides guests with an outdoor pool. Free WiFi is included.

At the hotel, every room is equipped with a wardrobe. The private bathroom is equipped with a bath. At Mitchell Grand Hotel every room comes with air conditioning and a flat-screen TV

Colombo is 14 km from Mitchell Grand Hotel, while Negombo is 45 km away. The nearest airport is Bandaranaike International Airport, 38 km from the hotel.
